“Revelation”—Event and Knowledge

Revelation of

    1. The righteous judgment of God (by Jesus Christ) [Rom 2:5]—event
    2. Glory in us [Rom 8:18; 1Pe 5:1]—event
    3. The day of the Lord, by fire[1Co 3:13]—event
    4. The quality of every man’s work, by fire [1Co 3:13]—event
    5. Jesus Christ with his mighty angels from heaven [2Th 1:7; Luk 17:30; 1Pe 1:13]—event
    6. The man of sin, the son of perdition, the wicked, in his time [2Th 2:3,6,8]—event
    7. The Father by Christ [Mat 11:27]—event
    8. Salvation in the last time[1Pe 1:5]—event
    9. The wrath of God from heaven [Rom1:18]
    10. God’s righteousness that is by faith through the Gospel [Rom 1:17]—knowledge
    11. The mystery, which was kept secret since the world began [Rom 16:25]—knowledge
    12. What God has prepared for those who love him, by the Holy Spirit [1Co 2:9-10]—knowledge
    13. Anything, to prophets [1Co 14:30]—knowledge
    14. The mystery of Christ in the Gospel which Paul preached, by Christ [Gal 1:12; Eph 3:3-5]—knowledge
    15. Christ in Paul [Gal 1:16]—knowledge
    16. Faith in Christ [Gal 3:23]—knowledge
    17. Deep things [Mat 11:25]—knowledge
    18. Christ, by the Father [Mat 16:17]—knowledge
    19. Satan’s fall from heaven like lightning [Luk 10:21,18]—knowledge
    20. The arm of the Lord [Joh 12:38]—knowledge
    21. The Book of Revelation [Rev 1:1]—knowledge
