A Few Lessons from Job 13

  • To speak when there is nothing to say I foolishness and it is wisdom to keep silent when there is nothing to say [Job 13:5].
  • We are not meant to lie to defend God, for He will not praise us for doing so, but will punish [Job 13:7-11].

Re-examining the Doctrine of the Trinity

  1. The mandate of Nebuchadnezzar as king of kings was the same Adam’s mandate. They were to have dominion over all the men, nations, and beasts [Dan 2:37-38].
  2. Everything that happens in this present age is by the decree of angels [Dan 4:17]. This age is ruled by angels.
