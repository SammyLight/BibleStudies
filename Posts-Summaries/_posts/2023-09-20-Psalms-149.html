Psalm 149

  1. We will sing a new song at the resurrection.
  2. Playing the tambourine and harp in church is prophesying of the resurrection [Psa 149:3].
  3. Salvation is the garment of the priests. Our new bodies are called salvation/righteousness [Psa 149:3; 132:15,9].
  4. God is going to make the meek priests [Psa 149:3].
  5. The saints will carry out the judgment decreed against the princes (spiritual and physical) of this age [Psa 149:5-9].
  6. Though we will not have blood after the resurrection, we will have breath [Psa 150:6].
