Comments Section

Prophet

Prophets don't get anything from God beyond what is possible by creation or by inheritance from their fathers.
God's relationship with the prophet only makes a dormant blessing active, it doesn't really add anything new to the prophet.

Father

Father's get from God what is only possible by creation.
To inherit blessings from a father one must be his son or bondservant. One must be a part of his family.
